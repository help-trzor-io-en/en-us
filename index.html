What Is Trézór?

Trézór is a hardware wallet developed by SatoshiLabs that allows users to store cryptocurrencies offline. Unlike software wallets that are vulnerable to online attacks, the Trézór hardware wallet provides cold storage—keeping your private keys offline and in your control.

Whether you own Bitcoin, Ethereum, or thousands of other supported tokens, Trézór ensures your funds are protected with advanced encryption and two-factor authentication.

🧭 Ready to start? Visit Trézór.io/start® to begin setting up your device.

🚀 Getting Started with Trézór.io/start®

To start using your Trézór wallet, follow these simple steps on the official onboarding site: Trézór.io/start®

  1. Choose Your Device: Trézór offers two models—Trézór Model One and Trézór Model T. Select the one you have.

  2. Install Trézór Bridge: This software acts as a communication link between your computer and the Trézór hardware wallet.

  3. Connect Your Device: Plug in your Trézór via USB and follow the on-screen instructions.

  4. Initialize Wallet: Create a new wallet and write down the 12- or 24-word recovery seed.

  5. Set PIN Code: This protects your wallet from unauthorized access.

  6. Access Trézór Suite: The companion app, Trézór Suite, allows you to send, receive, and manage crypto securely.

🔒 Security Benefits of Trézór Hardware Wallet

Choosing a Trézór hardware wallet gives you an edge when it comes to digital asset security:

  • Cold Storage: Keeps private keys offline

  • Open-Source Firmware: Audited by the community for transparency

  • Secure Recovery: Restore your wallet safely using your seed phrase

  • Physical Confirmation: Transactions require manual approval on the device

  • Shamir Backup: Optional feature for advanced users to split the seed into multiple shares

🧠 Pro Tips for Trézór Beginners

  • Never store your seed phrase digitally. Write it down and store it in a fireproof safe.

  • Use a strong PIN code. Avoid common sequences like 1234.

  • Always verify firmware. Make sure it’s downloaded from official sources.

  • Practice using Trézór Suite. Familiarize yourself with its features before transferring large amounts.
